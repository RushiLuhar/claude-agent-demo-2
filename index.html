About Yash Shah

Yash Shah is a distinguished technology leader and entrepreneur with over two decades of experience in the IT and software industry. As the Co-founder and Managing Director of Jeavio, he leads a venture services firm that specializes in accelerating product development for early-stage technology companies and driving digital transformation for B2B SaaS enterprises.

Throughout his career, Yash has demonstrated exceptional leadership abilities and a commitment to fostering innovation in the technology sector. His expertise spans across various domains, including product development, technology strategy, and business transformation. Before founding Jeavio, he served as the CTO of SevOne and held positions at prominent organizations including IBM.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Yash is deeply involved in the entrepreneurial ecosystem. He has served as the President of TiE Boston and was elected to the TiE Global Board of Trustees, where he continues to mentor and support emerging entrepreneurs. His role as an angel investor and philanthropist further demonstrates his commitment to giving back to the community and fostering innovation.

Yash holds a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Clarkson University and is based in Boston, Massachusetts. His combination of technical expertise, business acumen, and dedication to entrepreneurship makes him a respected figure in the technology industry.
